First Aid For A Drug Allergy

    1. Call 911 immediately if the person has:

  • Sudden development of itching and hives
  • Swollen lips,tongue or throat
  • Hoarseness of voice or trouble speaking
  • Tightness in the throat or a feeling that the airways are closing
  • Had severe reaction in the past

    • 2. Stop taking the drug that triggered the reaction

    3. Control itching

  • Give an Antihistamine
  • Stay in a cool room
  • For a rash, apply steroid cream

    • 4. Go see a doctor if symptoms get worse
